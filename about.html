About
Zenrise.

We are a two-person walking tour business based in Fujisawa. We design thoughtful, locally-led walking tours through the lesser-known corners of the Shonan coast.

Founded in 2024, we're on a journey to discover and share the hidden gems of Japan — the quiet, beautiful places that travellers usually miss.
