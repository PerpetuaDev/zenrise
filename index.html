About us

A slower
way to see
Japan.

Founded in Kamakura, 2019

Zenrise was started by two friends — one a tea ceremony apprentice, one a tired travel editor — who kept finding the country they loved hiding just off the main route.

We design unhurried journeys for travelers who’d rather stay one night longer than rush to the next stop. Every itinerary is led by a small team who actually lives where we send you — chefs, monks, ceramicists, fishermen.

Groups never exceed six. The mornings are long. The trains are local. The point isn’t to see more of Japan, but to see Japan more closely.

01

Slow by design

Two to three places per trip, never the highlight reel. Walking when we can, local trains when we can’t.

02

Hosted by locals

Every day is led by someone whose family has lived in that town for at least three generations.

03

Small groups

Maximum six travelers. Often four. Sometimes just you and a guide.
